The Story

A marine biologist returns to the town where her grandmother vanished fifty years ago. What she finds in the water is poison. What she finds in the town is silence. What she finds in herself is a question she's been avoiding for six years.

The Saltmere Chronicles is a twelve-chapter literary mystery about generational secrets, environmental crime, and the weight of complicity. Set in a dying Pacific Northwest fishing village, it follows Sarah Chen as she uncovers a conspiracy that has shaped three generations—and discovers that some of the town's founding families will kill again to keep it hidden.

About

The Saltmere Chronicles was created using AI-assisted writing and voice synthesis—not as a gimmick, but as a genuine creative collaboration. The story, characters, and themes emerged from iterative work between human vision and machine capability.

This is literary fiction that happens to use new tools. The goal was never "AI-generated content" but a story worth telling, told well.
